Driver Controller

a - IntakeCommand

Left Trigger - Note Aim  (TeleopDriveWithVision)

Right Trigger - April Tag 

Right Bumper - Swap forward direction


Partner controller controls #########################


a - ShootCommand

x - IntakeCommand

y - StopIndexAndShooter

b - EjectNote

Right Trigger - Climber Up

Left Trigger - Climber Down

rightBumper - Climber up for 10 seconds

leftBumper - Climber down for 10 seconds
